We Protect Your Systems

Your servers are under attack within seconds after they connect to the Internet.

If compromised, the average time to discovery in 2014/2015 was 207 days.  But as the graph to the right shows, the average compromise of a system that could be measured in ‘days’ has steadily increased from 75% upwards, yet the compromises discovered in ‘days’ as a percentage is still below 25%.

Some companies can afford to dedicate highly qualified Systems Administrators, keep them trained in the latest security threats and ensure they have qualified cover during holiday and other breaks. For the rest of us, there’s Matta 360.  We take on the myriad of roles that would otherwise require several dedicated full time resources.

Managing your online systems is more complex than ever before.  The consequences of getting it wrong are increasingly severe because there are now many ways to monatize a compromised system, from identify theft, sale of data, diversion of funds,  ransomware and others.  Data Protection is becoming more stringent, and regulated industries in particular are under pressure.

Security companies themselves have always been a target of the hacking elite.  Claiming the scalp of a security company is a major boast for any hacking group wanting to make a name for themselves.  So security company’s are a pretty paranoid bunch.  Matta is the first UK security consultancy to offer a complete and holistic package of managed systems security.  We know that it has to be a complete solution, otherwise it’s worthless – like nobody would want to fly in a jumbo jet with missing parts, systems security has to be a complete solution.

We don’t try to dazzle you with a long shopping list of products included.  We have a feature table here – which covers some of the important things we do but the important point is – that you don’t need to care whether we have an IPS or an IDS, what malware protection we use, and whether we configure a tripwire… The answer is ‘yes’.  We protect you as we protect ourselves, and our reputation.  When you take on our 360 service, it’s like going on holiday – the first few days, you’re still wound up in the day to day anxiety, but pretty soon you can just relax!
