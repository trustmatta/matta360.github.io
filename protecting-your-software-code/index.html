We Protect Your Code

Your software runs your business. Protect it.

That source code is the representation of your ideas, unique concepts and way that you engage with your clients. But because it’s intangible, you may not realise its at risk.

If you have developed software that your business uses, then you have an important asset.  It’s usually an asset that goes unrecognised and unfortunately unprotected. Until something bad happens.

  • A dispute with your third party developers, your code may be held ransom
  • If you don’t control your codebase, it can, (and does) leak into other projects, including to your competitors
  • Your developer goes bust, you may never see your code again

Recognising that your code needs to be protected is the first step.  Ask yourself these questions:

  • Does the code your developers gave you build correctly?
  • Is it feature-complete?
  • How do you control who has access to the codebase?
  • Are the developers storing ‘secrets’ in the code?
  • Does it seem like when they fix one thing, it breaks another?
  • Is your code managed in a way that supports quick and elegant fall back to an earlier version?
